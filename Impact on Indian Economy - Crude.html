Headline: Every $10/bbl rise adds ~55–60 bps to India's CPI. At $112.57 — a $45.57 rise from Jan's $67 base — crude alone adds ~250 bps. Combined with rupee sliding from ₹86.8 to ₹94.71 (adding ~66 bps), India's total inflation shock is ~315 bps, pushing CPI toward 5.4–5.9%. RBI's upper tolerance band is 6%.
